About Us

Discover the story behind the Apple Watch and the passionate team driving innovation.

Our Mission

At Apple, our mission is to create technology that empowers people to improve their lives and make the impossible possible. We strive to push the boundaries of innovation, designing products that inspire creativity, foster connectivity, and enhance the quality of life.

The Apple Watch is a testament to this mission. It's more than just a smartwatch—it's a personal companion designed to keep you healthy, productive, and connected. With cutting-edge features like advanced health monitoring, seamless integration with your digital ecosystem, and unparalleled accessibility, the Apple Watch brings a perfect blend of technology and humanity.

We believe technology should be intuitive, accessible, and empowering for everyone. Our commitment is to continue shaping a future where people can achieve more with the tools we create, transforming the way we live, work, and interact.

Our Story

Founded in 1976, Apple has been a pioneer in redefining technology and how it integrates into everyday life. From the introduction of the Macintosh, which revolutionized personal computing, to the iPhone, which transformed communication and set a new standard for smartphones, Apple has consistently pushed the boundaries of what’s possible.

The Apple Watch is the latest chapter in our journey of innovation—a product that represents the culmination of decades of technological breakthroughs, meticulous design, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Designed to be more than just a timepiece, the Apple Watch is a personal companion that brings together health, fitness, communication, and style.

Our story is not just about creating products; it's about empowering people to achieve more and live better lives. With each innovation, we aim to build technology that inspires and connects, always keeping our users at the heart of everything we do. The Apple Watch is a symbol of our mission to combine technology and humanity, creating tools that truly enhance the way we live.

Meet the Team

Tim Cook

Chief Executive Officer

Tim Cook has been leading Apple since 2011 as its CEO, taking the reins from Steve Jobs. Under his leadership, Apple has grown into one of the most valuable companies in the world, launching innovative products like the Apple Watch, AirPods, and the M1 chip. Tim is also known for championing sustainability and human rights, driving initiatives like Apple’s 100% renewable energy goal and strong commitments to diversity and inclusion.

Jeff Williams

Chief Operating Officer

Jeff Williams joined Apple in 1998 and has been integral to the company’s success. As COO, he oversees global operations, supply chain, and customer support. Jeff has played a key role in the development of the Apple Watch, helping transform it into the leading wearable device. Known for his strategic vision and operational excellence, Jeff continues to drive innovations that enhance customer experiences worldwide.

John Ternus

Senior Vice President, Hardware Engineering

John Ternus leads Apple’s hardware engineering team, which is responsible for designing and developing innovative hardware across the entire product lineup. Joining Apple in 2001, John has been instrumental in the creation of groundbreaking products like the iPad Pro, M1-powered Macs, and the latest Apple Watch. His technical expertise and leadership have cemented Apple’s position as a leader in cutting-edge technology and hardware.

Lisa Jackson

Vice President, Environment, Policy, and Social Initiatives

Lisa Jackson leads Apple’s ambitious environmental initiatives and policies, focusing on sustainability and social impact. Under her leadership, Apple has become a global leader in renewable energy and responsible sourcing of materials. She also oversees the company’s ambitious goal to make every product carbon neutral by 2030. Her efforts have led Apple to reduce carbon emissions and integrate environmental considerations into product's life cycle.

Craig Federighi

Senior Vice President, Software Engineering

Craig Federighi is responsible for overseeing the development of Apple's iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and watchOS operating systems. His leadership has been instrumental in the evolution of Apple's software ecosystem, focusing on making the user experience seamless and intuitive across all Apple devices. Federighi’s team has pioneered innovations like Siri, iCloud, and Face ID. He has also been a key player in Apple's transition to custom silicon chips, like the M1 and M2, making Apple devices more powerful.

Deirdre O'Brien

Senior Vice President, Retail + People

Deirdre O'Brien is responsible for overseeing Apple’s global retail operations and human resources. With a focus on more than 500 retail locations worldwide, she plays a key role in shaping Apple’s customer experience and driving business growth. O’Brien also leads talent acquisition and development, ensuring employees thrive. Under her leadership, Apple’s retail stores have successfully embraced digital transformation, ensuring they remain at the forefront of customer engagement and innovation.
